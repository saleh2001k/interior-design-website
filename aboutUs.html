About us

We started HELLO CREATIVITY in 2021 because as design enthusiasts making a home in Brussels, we struggled to find affordable and locally accessible furniture and homewares that truly reflected our own style and character Furnishing your home can be quite overwhelming at times, at least that's how it was for us.

HELLO CREATIVITY was born to help others going through the maze that is interior design.

We decided to curate interior design modern trends, inspirations, tips and tricks from the vast interior collective to help you bring your home to life.
